Installation & Maintenance

Placement, Maintenance, and Installation

Where to Mount

Mount the indoor unit high on the longest wall to improve throw and coverage across the garage.
Aim airflow toward the occupied area, but not directly at people for long sessions.
Keep the unit away from dust-heavy cutting or grinding zones whenever possible.
Choose a location that allows a clean condensate drain path and sensible line-set routing.

Owner Tips

1
Pre-condition the garage about 15-20 minutes before starting work or a workout.
2
Clean filters every 2-3 weeks if the garage doubles as a dusty workshop.
3
Insulate the garage door first because it usually has the biggest comfort payoff.
4
Keep the outdoor unit clear of leaves, clutter, snow, and anything that restricts airflow.

Before Installation

Gravity Drain vs Pump

Use a gravity drain when possible. Add a condensate pump only if the layout cannot support a downhill route.

Dust and Filter Care

Woodworking and sanding garages need more frequent filter cleaning and often benefit from a pre-filter approach.

DIY vs Pro Install

A professional install helps with refrigerant handling, vacuum procedure, code, and cleaner finishing details.

Electrical Check

Many systems require a dedicated 115V or 230V circuit. Do not assume the same circuit as larger shop tools.

Common Questions

Garage Mini Split FAQ

这里也整体抬高了字号和对比度，FAQ 终于会像“能读”的电商 FAQ，而不是脚注。

Is a ductless mini split a good option for a garage?

+
Yes. A ductless system works especially well in garages because it avoids bulky ductwork, conditions the room only when needed, and gives a much cleaner retrofit path than traditional central air.

What size mini split do I need for a 2-car garage?

+
A 2-car garage often starts around 12,000-18,000 BTU. If the garage is poorly insulated, oversized vertically, or used as a workshop, sizing up is usually the safer recommendation.

Can a mini split heat an uninsulated garage?

+
It can, but performance and efficiency will suffer. The better shopper experience is to recommend insulation first, then match BTU size and cold-climate capability appropriately.

How much does a garage mini split usually cost?

+
On this page, the showcased garage-friendly systems run roughly from the $700 range to a little over $1,000 depending on BTU, efficiency level, and whether Ultra Heat capability is included.

What is the best mini split for a workshop?

+
That depends on heat load and size, but workshops often need more capacity than a standard parking garage. An 18K system is a common starting point, with high-efficiency or cold-climate upgrades depending on use.
